01 — Naval Architecture

Hull Design &
Performance Engineering

Hydrostatics · CFD · Performance Predictions

Race-proven hull design for high-performance catamarans and monohulls. We adapt offshore-proven elements — steps, pads, compression tunnels, notched transoms — to vessels that carry more weight on less power.

  • High-performance catamaran & monohull specialization
  • Hydrostatics: weight, waterlines & trim analysis
  • CFD validation — Orca with Simerics
  • Performance predictions: engine packages & load conditions
  • Reverse/drop chines, lateral stability elements
02 — Design Studio

Surface Modeling &
Design Development

Class-A Surfacing · Composite-Aware · Digital Twin

Refined design blending high performance with classic detail. Our surface modeling accounts for composite behavior after lamination — designing out concavity before it becomes a problem in the mold.

  • Class-A surfacing refined from every angle
  • Composite-aware modeling (post-lamination shrink designed out)
  • Innovative joint methodology: intuitive bonding & assembly
  • Articulating assemblies, pantographic hinges, weldments
  • Full CAD model management for seamless team collaboration
